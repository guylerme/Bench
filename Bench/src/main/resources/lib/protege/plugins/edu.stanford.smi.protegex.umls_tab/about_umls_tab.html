UMLS Tab

The UMLS tab allows users to search the National Library of Medicine's Unified Medical Language System (http://www.nlm.nih.gov/research/umls/umlsmain.html) and annotate Protégé ontologies with terms, concept ids, synonyms, relations, and other information from the UMLS.

For more information, please refer to the UMLS Tab page on the Protégé Wiki.
